Pick Up & Delivery

Free pick-up and delivery from your office or home

For Pick-up and Delivery service, contact us to book your pick-up and delivery services. Our staff from Khwiz Laundry will come to pick-up your clothing.

If we are picking your garments from the office, decide where your pick-up and drop off point will be (usually the reception at your place of work).

How Does Pick-up and Delivery Service Work?

  • Contact us to book your pick-up and delivery services.
  • Simply place your dry cleaning in a plastic bag along with your personal contact details as well as the invoice number.
    Note: Payment is recommended before the driver from Khwiz Laundry come, so ensure your payment is done to M-pesa till no. 168621.
  • Place the bag in the pre-arranged pick-up point. Khwiz Laundry pick-up laundry and dry cleaning from your nominated pick-up point as from 9am that is Monday to Friday.
  • Khwiz Laundry deliver your dry cleaning to your drop off point before 4pm the next business day (Monday to Friday).

Reasons to dry clean

There are many reasons to have your clothing cleaned with us:

Do away with the Iron: Ironing is not enjoyable to everyone. Khwiz Laundry professional finishing means clothing get a wrinkle-free and new look appearance.

Removal of Stains: We use multifaceted procedures and special stain removal chemicals to remove stains. There are two major categories: solvent-soluble stains and water-soluble stains. These different stains require different treatments, which our stain removal technicians are trained to administer.

Fabric Care: Each fabric requires a different kind of laundering treatment. We have this knowledge and apply our skills to care best for the clothing we receive.

Long Lasting Clothes: Regular dry cleaning can prolong the life of certain clothing because clothing is being treated and cared for professionally and accordingly. Dry cleaning your clothing regularly helps you to protect your clothes and inevitably your appearance.

Avoid Shrinkage: Shrinkage is the most common reason why good clothing is destroyed. Dry cleaning avoids any shrinkage of any form.

Look & Feel Better: Special dressing equipment, professional finishing and our dry cleaners' attention to detail, clothing will have a wrinkle-free and like-new appearance. Looking good makes everyone feel better and confident. Khwiz Laundry press your clothes for you to impress.

Safeguards from Moths: Cleaned clothing prevents moth and other insect damage. Insects can damage clothes through direct damage which is caused by a group of insects feeding directly on a fabric and indirect damage is caused by insects as they feed on spilled food or perspiration on the fabric. Moths attack the clothing directly, especially wool and wool blends.
